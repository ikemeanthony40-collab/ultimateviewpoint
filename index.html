Meet the Author & Platform

Anthony Mbadiwe
Ikeme

Analytical Author · Digital Creator · Agribusiness Entrepreneur

Ultimate Viewpoint is a platform dedicated to the honest evaluation of the issues, ideas, and principles that shape our world. As a digital creator, educator, and devout Christian, Anthony Mbadiwe Ikeme provides deep, independent insights into human, religious, economic, and political matters — without compromise or agenda.

A graduate of Michael Okpara University of Agriculture, Umudike with a Second Class Upper degree in Agribusiness and Management, and a 2025 certified AI and Engineering Prompt professional through Certiport and the Computer Professionals of Nigeria, Anthony brings both academic rigour and technological expertise to everything published here.
