Meet the Author

Anthony Mbadiwe
Ikeme

Analytical Author · Digital Creator · Agribusiness Entrepreneur

Ultimate Viewpoint is a platform dedicated to the honest evaluation of the issues, ideas, and principles that shape our world. As a digital creator, educator, and devout Christian, Anthony Mbadiwe Ikeme's mission is to provide deep, independent insights into human, religious, economic, and political matters — without compromise.

A graduate of Michael Okpara University of Agriculture, Umudike with a Second Class Upper degree in Agribusiness and Management, Anthony brings a rigorous academic foundation to his writing. His additional certification in Peace and Conflict Resolution reflects a deep commitment to understanding how societies fracture and heal.

A 2025 certified AI and Engineering Prompt professional through Certiport and the Computer Professionals of Nigeria, Anthony is at the frontier of how digital technology intersects with intellectual creation. Through his digital books and content, he challenges perspectives, encourages critical thinking, and equips readers with frameworks for navigating an increasingly complex world.
